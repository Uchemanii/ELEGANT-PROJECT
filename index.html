Nairaland
Nairaland is an ultimate Nigerian platform that helps you stay in the know of everything happening in Nigeria getting you up-to-date news and information from over 2.6 million registered users & 6.91 million published topics ranging from politics to sports music and more!
Understanding the problem
I conducted my research on revamping a large platform like nairaland and digested the necessary quality of material needed to improve its users. During this research, which I conducted using Twitter and Google form I got to find out majority of its users were in their mid-30’s and 40’s, and younger generation hardly makes use of or spends longer time on the platform, so this prompted me to work on the user interface to drive in more users.
Background
Goal
The goal of this challenge is to enhance the user interface of such that it will be simple and easy for younger generations to use while maintaining its information architecture.
Role
Product designer
Careers page
Design process
Pain points
  1. Hidden CTA buttons:
The call-to-action buttons, like the login and create post buttons, are hardly noticed and not easily seen.
2. Spacing
There is no proper spacing in the comments section, and reactions to posts are so close that it might cause some sort of confusion.
3. Post
Whenever a user drops a post, it would capture a larger audience if there were a little bit of context, making every story more attractive to other users and visitors.
